Chicken

The chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus), a subspecies of the red junglefowl,
is a type of domesticated fowl, originally from Southeastern Asia.
Historically, a chicken was any young domesticated bird, and the species was called domestic fowl or fowl.[20]
